Privacy Policy

📋 Overview

FluxLaneX ("the App") is developed by Bonepau. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

📦 Data We Collect

FluxLaneX does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely offline. The only data stored is:

  • Your high score (stored locally on your device)
  • Music on/off preference (stored locally on your device)

This data never leaves your device and is stored using Android's SharedPreferences system.

🌐 Internet & Permissions

FluxLaneX requires no internet connection and requests no device permissions. The App functions fully offline.

🧒 Children's Privacy

FluxLaneX does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13. The App is safe for all ages.

🔗 Third-Party Services

FluxLaneX does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date below. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
